Incrementality

Search is a harvester: one always-on intent placement takes 72% of connections and closes journeys that social opens (34 Meta-first and 19 TikTok-first paths ended on Search). Meta is the clearest demand CREATOR — its wallet volume (14,609) and the Feb wallet surge coinciding with its flight ramp show genuine lift, and it still pays on its own attribution (ROI 3.1–10.7). TikTok Traffic creates at breakeven-plus. VOD, YouTube brand, HI Display and LinkedIn show no wallet-creation lift in the weekly pattern around their flights despite ~$433k of spend, so their 'awareness feeds Search' defence is not supported by the data. Confidence: medium — no view-through data and one tag limit the path evidence.

Funnel

GA4 is flagged as loaded (ga4Loaded=true) but the funnel, weekly events and campaign-session arrays are all empty in this extract, so the landing→balance leak cannot be priced. The proxy signal is damning anyway: HI Display bought 772k clicks for 369 engaged users and 21 connections — that traffic is almost certainly bouncing before identification. Ask for the GA4 funnel by channel before the next flight is signed off.

CRM overlap

Two stim windows overlap media: Apr 1–29 and Jun 9–30 (At Risk GPT stims). The June window covers 3 of 4 June weeks, exactly when connections ticked UP (68 and 60 in weeks of Jun 15/22) while wallet creations fell — some of those connections likely belong to CRM, not paid media, which flatters June media further than it deserves (and June media is already failing on its own numbers). Do not credit the June burst with the late-June connection uptick; the April window similarly softens confidence in April cohort attribution.

Maturation

Median days-to-convert is 4 and 56% of connections land within 7 days, but the tail matters: Feb's cohort grew from 250 (d30) to 374 (d90), a further +50%. Jan–Mar cohorts are fully matured and safe to judge. May is immature (d60=d90 at 221 — the d90 window hasn't closed) and June is only ~30 days old (212 connections so far, plausibly ~310+ at maturity). June wallet counts ARE final (wallets tag instantly), so zero-wallet June placements are safely condemned; June CONNECTION verdicts should be re-read in late September when the cohort hits d90.
