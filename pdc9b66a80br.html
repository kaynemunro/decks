Where the value comes from

Four drivers, all observed in production.

~$10.8m/yr

Reduced churn on the engaged base

~200k engaged customers churn 4pp lower than matched control — roughly 8,000 customers retained a year.

8,000 × $1,350 CLV = $10.8m/yr

~$5.7m/yr

At-risk save campaigns

50–100k at-risk customers targeted monthly, delivering 200–500 saves a month.

4,200 saves/yr × $1,350 = $5.7m/yr

~$8.9m/yr

WhatsInMyWallet acquisition

~5,000 wallets created a month → ~300 month-1 connections, doubling over months 2–5 (~550).

≈$740k lifetime value per $90k month of media (≈8× media ROI) → ≈$8.9m/yr

2×+ attach

Quality of acquired customers

Broadband attach >10% and second-mobile attach ~48% — over double standard acquisition. Multi-product cohorts churn at roughly half the rate of a single-line customer.
