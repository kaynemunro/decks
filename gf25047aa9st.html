The mechanics

How the fund works

Simple on purpose. You're only ever chasing your own best.

Step 01

$300 to open

Every rep started the trial with $300 sitting in their own fund, ready to go from day one.

Step 02

+$300 per extra sale

Every acquisition sale above your own weekly baseline tops the fund up by another $300. Your baseline, your target.

Step 03

Deploy it to close

Load it at point of sale as Phone Dollars to get the deal in front of you over the line.

● Always use the  Growth Fund reason code  — it's how your deployments get counted, so you get full credit for every one.

Your performance is always measured against your own baseline — not anyone else's. At store level we also sanity-check the programme against door-count conversion vs other stores, so busy or quiet weeks never distort your read.

What we learned

Three things the month taught us

01

Fund use and lift go together

The reps who deployed the fund were, week after week, the reps beating their own baseline. Sylvia Park had 11 of 13 using it — and finished 21% up.

02

It works from any starting point

Big baselines, tiny baselines, brand-new reps — the fund delivered wherever it was used. Saad proved it at Albany; Shuai proved it at Sylvia Park.

03

The reason code is your friend

Lots of what you load are legitimate service credits — competitor matches, remediation. The Growth Fund reason code keeps your fund work visible so you get full credit for it. Use it every time.

One honest note: usage wasn't consistent everywhere — so we'll be checking in with a few of you to make sure everyone feels confident using the fund. If it feels unclear, that's on us, not you.
