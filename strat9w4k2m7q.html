01 · The one idea

Our moat isn't the boost. It's the extra connection.

Multi-connection customers churn at ~3.3% a year vs ~31% for mobile-only — a 10× retention gap. Spark and 2degrees can match a device discount; neither can match a wallet that compounds in value with every connection you add. That is the battleground we win.

06 · The five moves

Five things to do differently.

1
Activate the wallet before spending another dollar on boosts.

~80% of boosted customers never open the wallet. Crucially — Phone Dollars are a temporary balance top-up, not a perpetual giveaway: if they don't redeem, it expires and costs us nothing. So this isn't wasted money — it's an unrealised retention asset at no extra risk. We only "pay" when they engage, and engagement is exactly what drives stickiness. Re-architect the journey to force first-open → first-redemption; put the balance on the app home, the bill, an SMS.

2
Convert the device sale into a connected home — at the IFP checkout.

53% of IFP buyers land mobile-only (our worst funnel). Put a CMGM moment at point of sale: "add a line / broadband now, get $X Phone Dollars." Turn device-discount volume into 3.3%-churn households.

3
Make CMGM the spearhead — and add a broadband tier.

The $500 moved mobile but not broadband — the stickiest, most differentiated attach. Test a broadband-specific CMGM (bigger boost / first-months credit). Decide if $500 becomes a permanent step-up, funded by the churn it saves (a multi-connection save ≈ 10× a single-line one).

07 · Move 4 — the most actionable thing we can do NOW

Pre-load the Spark price-rise switch.

The opening

Spark confirmed mobile + broadband price rises for 31 July. Their base will shop Jun–Aug — a once-a-year retention crack.

We already hold the audience

~110–120,000 prospects on file, of which at least a third are Spark customers (~37–40k) — warm, identified, and about to become price-sensitive.

The play

Retarget those Spark prospects now, ahead of the rise: "switch, keep your device deal, and start a wallet that grows as you connect more." Warm + timely + on our terms — value, not discount depth.

Target multi-line / household potential — don't import Spark's single-line churn.
08 · The five moves (cont.)

Protect the base, fix the read.

5
Split collections churn out of everything — it's a payment problem, not a value one.

Our highest-churn cohort (early-tenure) is ~75% Collections — involuntary non-payment a boost can't fix. Build a separate early-tenure payment-health track (bill smoothing, proactive support); reserve Phone Dollars for the voluntary churn it can actually move.

+
Re-engage the $0-wallet, never-redeemed pocket — our fastest-rising churn lever.

"Your $X is waiting — here's how to use it." The money's already there; they don't know or use it. And investigate the compressing engaged-vs-never dividend before it erodes the core story.

+
Restore weekly NPS.

Sentiment is stale since April and the theme feeds are empty — we're running the program half-blind on why customers leave.

09 · By lever

Same idea, every lever.

Proposition

"The connected home that pays you back." Lead with compounding household value, not a device line. Defend the premium (we own iPhone 17 Pro Max — don't discount where we already win).

Pricing

Keep gating device value to higher plans ($73–88) — that's quality — but justify the plan with stacked wallet value, not by discounting it. Higher plans → higher CMGM earn rates.

Marketing

Re-point media from shallow top-of-funnel curiosity to directed app/wallet activation. Out-value 2degrees' brand play; counter Spark on total household value, not discount depth.

Digital journeys

One job post-acquisition: first wallet-open → first redemption. Make "your next connection unlocks $X" a self-serve in-app upsell. Cut friction to add a 2nd line / broadband.

Lifecycle

Trigger CMGM at the right moments (post-activation, sibling line on account, pre-IFP-rolloff). Separate the credit-risk track from the value track.

vs Spark / 2degrees

Spark buys single-line low-ARPU volume — don't match the depth, take their price-rise switchers. 2degrees is brand-led & easing — hold and monitor.

10 · Stop · Watch

What to stop, and what to watch.

Stop

Spreading at-risk boosts on low-churn cohorts (~66% of spend went to a 0.77%-churn cohort). Chasing Spark's single-line discount depth — it buys the churn. Running the program with stale NPS.

Watch (leading indicators)

Wallet-open & first-redemption rate · additional-mobile AND broadband attach at acquisition · mobile-only share of new joins · voluntary vs collections churn split · port-in capture around 31-Jul.

Direction is high-confidence (workbench data); sizing is "test before you scale" — we're reasoning ahead of full margin/ARPU economics and the wallet-liability cost.

On the floor · The ask

Compete on the household, not the handset.

The wallet is a free, dormant retention asset. The extra connection is a 10× churn lever. Spark's price rise is a once-a-year door. Activate the first, sell the second, walk through the third — now.
