01 · Our purpose

Why the tribe exists

We exist to operate and drive One Wallet as a Membership Programme. Every stream in the tribe works towards the same outcome: members who stay longer, do more with us, and tell their mates — because their One Wallet keeps rewarding them.

♥

Drive the programme

Grow One Wallet membership and engagement — building Phone Dollar propositions, offers and partnerships that give every member a clear reason to stay and earn more with us.

✦

Design the experience

Make One Wallet the home of membership — simple, human experiences across every touchpoint, so joining, earning and spending Phone Dollars feels effortless.

▲

Operate it brilliantly

Run One Wallet at scale — the platforms, controls and insight that keep the programme safe, measurable and always improving across the base.
